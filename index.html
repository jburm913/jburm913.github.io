My name is Jena Burmeister and I am a Junior Biblical counseling major with a concentation on women's ministry. My passion is helping women with their struggles in life to see hope that only a relationship with Christ can provide. I love making people smile :) My dream career path is to have my own personal counseling practice in affiliation with my local church.

I was born in Louisianna and lived the first ten years of my life there. I am still very proud of my Cajun culture. I have a younger brother who plays ice hockey and an incredible mom and dad whom I love dearly. I have had the privalege of dating my best friend for the past 3 years and we plan on getting married when we finish our degrees. Fun fact about me is I love animals and I finally own my dream pet... a pet chipmunk named Rishi. Some of my hobbies include hiking, spending time at the lake, and I also enjoy snuggling up with a cup of hot tea and a book and/or movie. I am a member of the Nu Alpha Phi Flames and am so excited to have the opportunity to lead as Vice President next semester. I am also a Life Group leader in society and a Group Leader in the dorms.
